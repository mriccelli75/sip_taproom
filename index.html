Beers Currently Flowing

We are proud to source locally owned pittsburgh brews. Come enjoy all of pittsburgh's finest in one location.

Hard Lemonade (Can) - 5% ABV

This hard lemonade features the tangy sweetness of your favorite fresh-squeezed beverage with a little kick to create the ultimate summer drink.

Cinderlands Beer Company:

Dry Irish Stout - 4.0% abv

Classic dry stout brewed with roasted barley, premium English specialty malts, and the best Maris Otter in the world.

Tentacle Horizon - Brewed with Cryo Mosaic and NZ Rakau hops IPA - 7.1% ABV

Hop Farm:

No Space for Mother In Law - 7.5%

West Coast IPA dry hopped with Taiheke, Columbus, Citra and Mosaic hops giving this beer the pine, tangerine and slight peach characteristics we all know and love in a Westy while letting the malty backbone shine through.

Hazel Grove Brewing:

Better Than One - 7% ABV

A hint of carmel and rich malty flavors are balanced with undertones of dried plum and hops in this classic Belgian Dubbel

Old Thunder:

Yorkshire Stillage - 4.5% ABV

A classic Pale Ale brewed with Golden Promise heritage barley malt and a blend of hops from the UK. Expect gentle notes of citrus, black tea, and wildflower honey.

Parakeet Island - 6.8% ABV

A california-style IPA brewed with a blend of hops from New Zealand. Expect bold notes of lemon-lime, tropical fruits, and soft pine.

Rural City Brewing Co:

American Lager - 5.9% ABV

To Be FRANK....this beer is crisp and crushable! We brewed a nice light American Lager and then dry hopped it with ZAPPA hops to give it a spicy/tropical/piney aroma that will BE like music to the taste buds.

About SIP Taproom

SIP Taproom is located in Hartwood Plaza right around the corner from Hartwood Acres. The goal of SIP is simple; Provide great beers and wines in a relaxing environment. Unique in it's service, we have great taps on all the time and a hand picked selection of wines from all over the world. We will offer an eclectic but simple tapas like food menu that will change daily. Set behind hartwood acres, SIP will be a great addition to the fox chapel and bordering shaler/hampton community. Come by after a hike or bike ride for that post drink or bring family and friends for a relaxing evening with great drinks. Hope to see you soon!
