We are proud to source locally owned pittsburgh brews. Come enjoy all of pittsburgh's finest in one location.

- Grist House: West Coast IPA ABV: 7.3

With big aroma and initing bitterness, this beer is a mountain top crusher. Our new West Coast IPA, brewed with Citra, Amarillo, and Waimea will make you want to send that next clean line. 7.3%

- Hitchhiker: Conversion - Dry Hopped Blonde Ale 4.5% ABV

Brewed with Pilsner malt. Hopped lightly with Amarillo and dry hopped with a touch of Amarillo. Notes of Lemon peel, fresh bread, crackers, and blossoms.

- Hitchhiker: Myrcene - Double IPA 8.4% ABV

Brewed with Oats. Hopped with Sabro, Citra, and Mosaic. Notes of pineapple, cantaloupe, pine sap, mango, and peach.

-Eleventh Hour: Moon Series - Harvest Moon - IPA - New England • 6.8% ABV

The ninth in our Moon series, we finish off this quarter with the Harvest Moon. Kettle hopped with Denali then lightly dry hopping with Strata, Amarillo, Ekuanot and Denali. A heavy pineapple nose with tart passionfruit mingling through. Mandarin oranges, sweet peaches and sharp citrus with a smooth finish. Keep an eye out for these ones, releasing every full moon throughout 2021. Each will be a progressively hopped beer building on the last

- Eleventh Hour: Oktoberfest Festbier - 5.5% ABV

The ninth in our Moon series, we finish off this quarter with the Harvest Moon. A traditional Oktoberfest festbier, this is going to be lighter than a Marzen with a sweet cracker & crisp finish. Smooth biscuit and a clean finish, you can picture yourself sitting under a tent in Munich with 10,000 of your closest friends. Enjoy a stein or two!

Available Wines

SIP's wine list is unique and hand selected to offer you the finest and hard to find wines from all over.

- Elouan Rose Blend Oregon

Refreshing aromas of strawberry, cantaloupe, and nectarine followed by notes of citrus. Lively flavors of grapefruit, strawberry, and nectarine fill the palate with subtle notes of lemon custard and kumquat. On the mid-palate, this wine is rich and expansive with bright acidity that carries flavors of raspberry and clementine rind through to a long-lasting, smooth finish

- Piper Sonoma Blanc de Blanc

Piper Sonoma Brut, made of 60% Chardonnay, 15% Pinot Noir and 25% Pinot Meunier, has a pale gold color, petite bubbles and delicate aromas of ripe apple, toast and peach. This sparkling wine is dry and fresh with a long elegant finish, possessing crisp citrus flavors with hints of strawberry and vanilla smoke

- Vinum Cabernet Sauvignon Insider Paso Robles Central Coast 2017

This Paso Robles Cabernet Sauvignon has a deep, dark, inky purple color. The aromas are comprised of concentrated blue fruit such as wild huckleberry and blueberries. The mid-palate is mouth coating with ripe cassis and juicy plum with spicy cinnamon-clove spice and generous blackberry flavor. The finish is silky smooth with balanced acidity, soft medium vanilla tannins and long boysenberry textured aria.

- Montinore Estate Of The Hills Pinot Noir Williamette Valley 2018

Established in 1982, Montinore Estate is the largest producer of certified estate wines made from Biodynamic® grapes in the country. With our 200-acre Demeter Certified Biodynamic® and Stellar Certified Organic vineyard located in north Willamette Valley in Oregon, we focus on producing superior Pinot Noirs, cool climate whites, and fascinating Italian

- Mason Cellars, Sauvignon Blanc Napa Valley

Our Sauvignon Blanc greets the nose with tropical fruit, figs, and honey. The palate offers creamy pear and fig flavors before giving way to a long, bright finish with hints of grapefruit.

- Avalon, Cabernet Sauvignon California

Juicy aromas of dark fruit, mocha and spice meet with hints of vanilla. On the palate, black cherry, red raspberry and plum are followed by a long, smooth finish.

- Artezin Wines, Zinfandel Mendocino County (2017)

Artezine Zinfandel is instantly recognizable as a classic, fruit-forward, Zinny Zin. This signature style offers a full-bodied wine with round tannins and momderate alcohol.

- Terra d'Oro, Zinfandel Amador County (2017)

This savory Zinfandel displays vibrant aromas of rich, spicy clove and big, generous fruit with hints of vanilla that entice the nose. An abundance of plums and flavors of dense, juicy blackberries excites the senses with a round mouthfeel of well integrated tannins on the finish.

- The Hess Collection, Chardonnay Shirtail Rances Monterey County

This elegant wine is rich with complex flavors of pear, apple, grapefruit, crisp, mild acidity, oak and vanilla.

- Montinore Estate, Pinot Gris Willamette Valley

This dry style Pino Gris begins with aromatics of bright citrus and tropical fruit that lead to a firm foundation of apple, mango and a hint of savory and spice on the palate, finish crisp and bright.

About SIP Taproom

SIP Taproom is located in Hartwood Plaza right around the corner from Hartwood Acres. The goal of SIP is simple; Provide great beers and wines in a relaxing environment. Unique in it's service, we have great taps on all the time and a hand picked selection of wines from all over the world. We will offer an eclectic but simple tapas like food menu that will change daily. Set behind hartwood acres, SIP will be a great addition to the fox chapel and bordering shaler/hampton community. Come by after a hike or bike ride for that post drink or bring family and friends for a relaxing evening with great drinks. Hope to see you soon!
