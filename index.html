About SIP Taproom

SIP Taproom is located in Hartwood Plaza right around the corner from Hartwood Acres. After a great bike ride or hike, stop by for that post ride beer or recharge with a craft beer while enjoying the company of local patrons from Fox Chapel, Shaler and surrounding area. We offer most local craft beers and a top notch selection of red and white wines. SIP's goal is to bring all together locals in a modern upscale but friendly environment. Hope to see you here at SIP.
